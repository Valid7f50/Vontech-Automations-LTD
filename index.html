Our Story

Built From Curiosity.
Driven By Purpose.

VonTech didn't start in a boardroom. It started with a young engineer who refused to wait for permission to learn. Armed with curiosity, determination, and a hands-on approach to solving real problems, the foundation of what would become VonTech Automation was laid one circuit, one line of code, and one project at a time.

What began as a personal journey into embedded systems and industrial automation grew into something bigger, a mission to make world-class engineering knowledge accessible to the next generation of Nigerian engineers. Because the founder learned by doing, every student and every client at VonTech gets that same philosophy: no shortcuts, no theory without practice, just real engineering.

Today, VonTech operates from Port Harcourt, delivering embedded systems, automation, and CAD solutions to businesses while simultaneously training students who go on to build careers and companies of their own.
